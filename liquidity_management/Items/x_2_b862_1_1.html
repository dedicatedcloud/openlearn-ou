1 Liquidity management

The following sections introduce and define liquidity risk, providing a series of explanatory contexts and scenarios to outline the strategies that organisations use to try and effectively manage it. A short case study towards the end of these sections illustrates some of the problems that can face companies characterised by inadequate levels of liquidity. In addition, some ideas about how organisations can access different credit facilities are discussed.
